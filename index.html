About Us

We are a team of students who created a website to help our peers connect with clubs and activities. Our site makes it easy to search, filter, and explore the many opportunities at FHS. From detailed club descriptions to personalized recommendations, our goal is to make it easy and fun to find clubs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to strengthen student involvement at FHS by removing barriers. Many students told us they wanted to join clubs but did not know where to start. By combining feedback from students and administrators we created a program that bridges that gap making a place where students can learn, grow, and belong.
